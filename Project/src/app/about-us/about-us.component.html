Vision

  • To be recognized by residents, employers and leaders of its served area as the preferred provider of health care services.
  • To be regarded as a model hospital, providing excellent primary, secondary and tertiary care services and integrating its programs with those of other health care organizations.
  • To promote the best possible care at the lowest possible cost.
  • To continuously strive to improve high quality inpatient, outpatient, emergency and long-term care services, and will seek to develop new ones in accordance with the community needs.
